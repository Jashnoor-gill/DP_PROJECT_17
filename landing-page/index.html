What is Releaf?
ReLeaf E-Waste is an initiative focused on responsible collection and segregation of electronic waste. Our mission is to bring relief to the environment by giving e-waste a new leaf of life through recycling, reuse, and safe disposal. By combining technology with sustainability, ReLeaf aims to reduce pollution, recover valuable resources, and create a greener future.
